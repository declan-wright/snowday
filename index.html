PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the RSU1 Snow Day Calculator, developed by seniors at Morse High School who created this app to help predict the likelihood of a snow day for RSU1 schools. The app is available on the web, Android, and iOS platforms. This Privacy Policy applies to all versions of the app.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Collection

The RSU1 Snow Day Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. The app uses fixed geographical coordinates to fetch publicly available weather data from the National Weather Service API. All calculations to determine the probability of a snow day are performed locally on your device.

Data Usage

The weather data retrieved from the National Weather Service API is used exclusively to calculate the probability of a snow day based on factors such as temperature, expected snowfall, and previous snowfall. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app utilizes the National Weather Service API to obtain weather forecasts. We do not share any user data with the National Weather Service or any other third-party services. The API requests include a User-Agent header to identify the app, but this does not involve any personal user information.

Local Storage

The app stores certain preference locally on your device or in your browser. This information is not accessible to us or any third parties and remains solely on your device to enhance your user experience.

Children's Privacy

The RSU1 Snow Day Calculator is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, there are no specific concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will notify you by updating the effective date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at snowdaycalculator@protonmail.com.
